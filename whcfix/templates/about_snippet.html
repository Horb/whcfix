About

This site was designed to answer the following question;

Where and when are all 12 of the Wakefield Hockey Club sides playing this weekend?

Previously this process could involve scouring several websites , (FixturesLive, YorkshireHA, Pitchero) to determine that information. Thankfully, whcfix.com does that searching for you.

Contributing

whcfix.com makes use of free and open technologies which you're more than welcome to contribute to on the github repository. Technologies include;

  • Flask, a Python web microframework.
  • Beautiful Soup for scraping data from other sites.
